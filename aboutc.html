About Our Store

E-Franchise is the name of our store which was founded in 2023. Our first product was chocolate milk, which we finally branded with the same name as our store. We sell the best chocolate milk with the best ingredients. Now we are starting to develop and add new products to our store. Among them are cakes, puddings, and totebags. In the future we will continue to release new products that make customers more and more satisfied.
